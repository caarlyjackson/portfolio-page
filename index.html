About Me

I am currently undertaking, and enjoying, the Sydney University & Trilogy Education Services Coding Boot Camp.
In my free time I enjoy writing and creating fictional characters, as well as detailed storylines for them and other supporting characters. I also like reading - books, quotes and philosophy, drawing, painting, and creating and editing photography for visual design/content in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator.
My personal preference in design and creativity leans towards genres featuring rebellion and chaos, and contratingly, moral lessons.
I appreciate art that forces the audience to contemplate the highlighted social justice, emotionality, or even taboo issue presented, and in turn, often disturb the comforted, and comfort the disturbed.
I like to classify myself and my work as Chaotic Good; rebellious yet highly moral.


Chaotic Good

Chaotic good is the best alignment you can be because it combines a good heart with a free spirit. Chaotic good can be a dangerous alignment when it disrupts the order of society and punishes those who do well for themselves.
